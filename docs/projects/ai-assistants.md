# Industrial AI Assistants

`2024 — present · DM-Solutions · AI Architect`

## Challenge

Production and industrial engineering workflows are full of expert
knowledge locked in documentation, regulations and the heads of senior
engineers. The goal: LLM-based assistants that make this knowledge
operational — without sending sensitive industrial data to external APIs.

## My role

- Designed AI assistants for industrial and production-engineering
  workflows.
- Architected the AI subsystem for developing predictive models.
- Designed deployment infrastructure for production operation of models.
- Led the model development and training teams.
- Adapted the architecture to security-compliance requirements (FSTEC).

## Architecture highlights

- **Agent orchestration** with tool integration: assistants query
  predictive models, documentation and enterprise systems rather than
  hallucinating answers.
- **On-premise LLM serving** — no external API dependency, full data
  locality.
- **Role-specific assistants**: a production engineer and an ML engineer
  get different capabilities on the same platform core.
